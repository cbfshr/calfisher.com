DuPont Pioneer Software Development Intern
During the Summer of 2014 (May 12th - August 7th), I worked at DuPont Pioneer in Johnston, Iowa as a Software Development Intern. I was put on a team responsible for certain applications used by agronomists, field managers, and temporary works in crop fields. These applications were web-based and geared towards being used mainly on tablet-sized touch screens. The purpose of the apps were to expedite and ease the process which the workers had to go through in order to inspect fields and save their observations.
