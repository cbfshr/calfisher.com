MicroCART
Fall 2015 - Spring 2016, Group Project - Senior Design
Senior Design is a two semester course intended to give engineering seniors a real-world project to better prepare them for the workplace after graduation. Our project is an Iowa State University-based research project that deals with a quadcopter with programmable logic. It introduces us to concepts about efficient hardware and software design, aerial concepts, and device-to-device communication.
